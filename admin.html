How-to guide 💡

Everything you need to run your shop — no tech skills needed!

🛍️ How to add a product
  1. Click Products in the sidebar
  2. Click + Add product
  3. Fill in the name, price, category and description
  4. Click the photo box and pick an image from your computer
  5. Click Save product — done! It appears in your shop straight away.
🗂️ How to add a category
  1. Click Categories in the sidebar
  2. Type the category name (e.g. Jewellery)
  3. Pick a fun emoji to represent it
  4. Click Add category — it appears as a filter button in your shop immediately!
📷 Tips for great product photos
  • Use natural light — near a window is perfect
  • A plain white or cream background looks the cleanest
  • Square photos (1:1 ratio) work best for product cards
  • Keep file sizes under 2MB so your site loads fast
🌐 How to put your site online (free!)
  1. Go to netlify.com and sign up free
  2. Drag your entire roomie-charm folder onto the Netlify dashboard
  3. Your site goes live immediately with a free link!
  4. You can connect a custom domain like roomie-charm.co.za later
